Worldwide Returns & Refunds Policy

There are a few important things to keep in mind when returning a product you purchased online from V E L:
• You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you purchased it*.
• Only items that have been purchased directly from V E L can be returned to V E L. V E L products purchased
through other retailers must be returned in accordance with their respective returns and refunds policy.
• Please ensure that the item you're returning is repackaged with all the papers, documents and products
that were included when you received it.

*in case of shipping delay, you can return your products within 90 days from the purchase if they comply
with the conditions of our policy.

There are some items, however, that are ineligible for return, including:
• Items with spots or signs
• Items with ruined or damaged fabric
• Items with ruined or damaged embroidery
• Items with ruined or damaged packaging
